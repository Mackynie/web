Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q: What is Bellen's Batchoyan? A: Bellen's Batchoyan is an e-commerce platform where you can order a variety of miryendas (snacks) and refreshments. We offer delicious and affordable items such as Batchoy, Pandesal, Halo-Halo, and various snacks and refreshments.
Q: How do I place an order? A: To place an order, browse through our products, select the items you wish to purchase, and click on the "Add to Cart" button. Once you have added all desired items to your cart, go to the cart page and proceed to checkout.
Q: How do I add items to my cart? A: To add items to your cart, simply click the "Add to Cart" button next to the item you wish to purchase. The item will be added to your cart, and you can view your cart by clicking on the cart icon at the top of the page.
Q: Can I remove items from my cart? A: Yes, you can remove items from your cart by clicking on the "Remove" button next to the item in your cart.
Q: What payment methods do you accept? A: We accept various payment methods, Gcash and cash on delivery.
Q: How long will it take to receive my order? A: Delivery times vary based on your location. Once your order is placed, you will receive an estimated delivery time. Typically, orders are delivered within minutes after the pickup.
Q: How can I contact customer support? A: You can contact our customer support team via email at support@bellensbatchoyan.com or by calling our hotline at (123) 456-7890. Our support team is available Monday to Friday, 9 AM to 6 PM.
